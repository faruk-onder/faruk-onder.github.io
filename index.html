About

Hello!
I am Faruk Önder, an experienced 3D artist and graphic designer in the mobile gaming industry. I enjoy working on creative projects and contributing to the world of entertainment through mobile games.
Using tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, Unity, and Blender, I produce a wide range of visual content, from in-game characters to environmental designs.
